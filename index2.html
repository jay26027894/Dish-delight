Breakfast

Poha

Ingredients: - 1 cup flattened rice (poha) - 1 small onion, finely chopped - 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional) - 1 medium potato, peeled and diced - 1/4 cup peanuts - 1/4 tsp mustard seeds - 1/4 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp sugar - 1 tbsp oil - Salt to taste - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish) - Lemon wedges (for serving) - Freshly grated coconut (optional, for garnish)
Instructions: 1. Preparation: - Rinse the poha under cold water in a colander until it softens. Drain and set aside. 2. Cooking: - Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. When they start to splutter, add the peanuts and sauté for a minute. - Add the chopped onions and green chilies (if using) to the pan. Sauté until the onions turn translucent. - Add the diced potato and cook until they become tender (about 5-7 minutes). You can cover the pan with a lid to speed up the cooking process. - Add turmeric powder, sugar, and salt. Stir well to combine. - Add the softened poha to the pan and mix gently to combine with the spices and vegetables. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally. 3. Garnish and Serve: - Turn off the heat and garnish with chopped coriander leaves and freshly grated coconut (if using). - Serve hot with lemon wedges on the side. Enjoy your poha!

Sandwich

Ingredients: - 4 slices of bread - 2 tbsp butter - 1 small cucumber, thinly sliced - 1 tomato, thinly sliced - 1 small onion, thinly sliced - 4-5 lettuce leaves - Salt and pepper to taste - 2 tbsp mayonnaise or any sandwich spread
Instructions: Preparation: Spread butter on one side of each bread slice. Spread mayonnaise or sandwich spread on the other side. Assembly: Place lettuce leaves on the buttered side of one slice of bread. Arrange cucumber, tomato, and onion slices over the lettuce. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste. Top with another slice of bread, mayonnaise side down. Serving: Cut the sandwich into halves or quarters. Serve immediately with chips or a side salad. Enjoy your sandwich!

Egg Toast

Ingredients: - 2 slices of bread - 2 eggs - 2 tbsp butter - Salt and pepper to taste - Optional: chopped herbs or cheese for garnish
Instructions: Preparation: Beat the eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper. Cooking: Heat butter in a pan over medium heat. Dip each slice of bread in the beaten eggs, ensuring both sides are coated. Place the bread in the pan and cook until golden brown on both sides. Garnish and Serve: Optionally, sprinkle with chopped herbs or grated cheese. Serve hot. Enjoy your egg toast!

Upma

Ingredients: - 1 cup semolina (rava) - 2 tbsp oil or ghee - 1 tsp mustard seeds - 1 tsp urad dal - 1 medium onion, finely chopped - 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped - 1 small carrot, finely chopped - 1/4 cup green peas - 2 cups water - Salt to taste - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish) - Lemon wedges (for serving)
Instructions: Preparation: Dry roast the semolina in a pan until it turns light golden. Set aside. Cooking: Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds and urad dal. When they start to splutter, add the chopped onions and green chilies. Sauté until the onions turn translucent. Add the chopped carrot and green peas, and cook for a few minutes. Add water and salt. Bring to a boil. Gradually add the roasted semolina, stirring continuously to avoid lumps. Cook until the mixture thickens and the semolina is cooked through. Garnish and Serve: Turn off the heat and garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with lemon wedges on the side. Enjoy your upma!

Dosa

Ingredients: - 1 cup rice - 1/2 cup urad dal - 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds - Salt to taste - Water (for soaking and grinding) - Oil or ghee (for cooking)
Instructions: Preparation: Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 4-6 hours. Drain and grind to a smooth batter. Add salt and let it ferment overnight. Cooking: Heat a non-stick tawa or griddle over medium heat. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges and cook until the dosa turns golden and crisp. Flip and cook for another minute. Serve: Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar. Enjoy your dosa!

Puri Bhaji

Ingredients: - For Puri: - 2 cups wheat flour - 1/2 tsp salt - Water (as needed) - Oil (for deep frying) For Bhaji: 3-4 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed 1 medium onion, finely chopped 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped 1 tsp mustard seeds 1/2 tsp turmeric powder 1 tbsp oil Salt to taste Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
Instructions: Preparation (Puri): Mix wheat flour and salt in a bowl. Add water gradually and knead into a stiff dough. Divide the dough into small balls and roll out into circles. Cooking (Puri): Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Fry the puris until they puff up and turn golden brown. Drain on paper towels. Preparation (Bhaji): Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and green chilies. When they start to splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until translucent. Add turmeric powder, salt, and mashed potatoes. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Garnish and Serve: Garnish the bhaji with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot puris with bhaji. Enjoy your puri bhaji!

Vada

Ingredients: - 1 cup urad dal - 1 small onion, finely chopped - 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped - 1 tsp grated ginger - A pinch of asafoetida (hing) - Salt to taste - Oil (for deep frying) - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
Instructions: Preparation: Soak urad dal in water for 4-6 hours. Drain and grind to a smooth batter. Add salt and let it ferment overnight. Cooking: Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Wet your hands, take a small portion of batter, shape it into a ball, and flatten it slightly. Fry the vadas until golden brown and crisp. Drain on paper towels. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar. Enjoy your vada!

Dhokla

Ingredients: - 1 cup gram flour (besan) - 1/2 cup yogurt - 1/2 cup water - 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste - 1 tsp lemon juice - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1 tsp fruit salt (Eno) - Salt to taste - 1 tbsp oil - 1 tsp mustard seeds - 1 tsp sesame seeds - A pinch of asafoetida (hing) - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish) - Grated coconut (for garnish)
Instructions: Preparation: Mix gram flour, yogurt, water, ginger-green chili paste, lemon juice, turmeric powder, and salt in a bowl to form a smooth batter. Add fruit salt and mix gently. Steaming: Pour the batter into a greased tray and steam for 15-20 minutes until cooked through. Let it cool slightly and cut into pieces. Tempering: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, sesame seeds, and asafoetida. Pour this tempering over the dhokla. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and grated coconut. Serve with chutney. Enjoy your dhokla!

Appam

Ingredients: - 1 cup raw rice - 1/4 cup cooked rice - 1/2 cup grated coconut - 1 tsp yeast - 2 tbsp sugar - Salt to taste - Water (as needed)
Instructions: Preparation: Soak the raw rice in water for 4-6 hours. Drain and grind with cooked rice, grated coconut, and water to form a smooth batter. Add yeast, sugar, and salt. Let it ferment overnight. Cooking: Heat an appam pan and pour a ladleful of batter. Swirl the pan to spread the batter thinly. Cover and cook until the edges are crisp and the center is soft. Serve: Serve hot with stew or coconut milk. Enjoy your appam!

Ingredients: - 2 cups whole wheat flour - 1/2 cup fenugreek leaves (methi), finely chopped - 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp red chili powder - 1/2 tsp carom seeds (ajwain) - Salt to taste - 2 tbsp oil - Water (as needed)
Instructions: Preparation: Mix all the ingredients in a bowl and knead into a soft dough. Divide the dough into small balls and roll out into thin circles. Cooking: Heat a tawa or griddle and cook each thepla on both sides until golden brown, using a little oil. Serve: Serve hot with yogurt or pickle. Enjoy your thepla!

Pancake

Ingredients: - 1 cup all-purpose flour - 1 tbsp sugar - 1 tsp baking powder - 1/2 tsp baking soda - A pinch of salt - 1 cup buttermilk - 1 egg - 2 tbsp melted butter - 1 tsp vanilla extract - Butter or oil (for cooking)
Instructions: Preparation: Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Combine the wet and dry ingredients to form a smooth batter. Cooking: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and grease with butter or oil. Pour a ladleful of batter and cook until bubbles form on the surface. Flip and cook until golden brown. Serve: Serve hot with syrup or your favorite toppings. Enjoy your pancakes!

Lunch

Butter Chicken

Ingredients: - 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into pieces - 1 cup plain yogurt - 1/2 cup cream - 2 tbsp butter - 1 large onion, finely chopped - 2 tomatoes, pureed - 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste - 1 tsp garam masala - 1 tsp red chili powder - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi) - Salt to taste - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish) - 2 tbsp oil
Instructions: 1. Marinate the Chicken: - In a bowl, mix yogurt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Add the chicken pieces and coat well. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour in the refrigerator. 2. Cooking: - Heat oil and butter in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. - Add onions and cook until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears. - Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and fenugreek leaves. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture. - Stir in garam masala and cream. Cook on low heat until the chicken is tender and the sauce is rich and creamy. 3. Garnish and Serve: - Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice. Enjoy your butter chicken!

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients: - 250 grams paneer, cubed - 1 cup tomato puree - 1/2 cup cream - 2 tbsp butter - 1 large onion, finely chopped - 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste - 1 tsp garam masala - 1 tsp red chili powder - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp coriander powder - Salt to taste - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish) - 1 tbsp oil
Instructions: 1. Cooking: - Heat oil and butter in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. - Add onions and cook until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant. - Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and coriander powder. Cook until the oil starts separating from the mixture. - Stir in garam masala and cream. Cook for a few minutes until the sauce is thickened. - Add paneer cubes and gently mix to coat them with the sauce. Cook for another 5 minutes. 2. Garnish and Serve: - Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice. Enjoy your paneer butter masala!

Biryani

Ingredients: - 1 cup basmati rice - 500 grams meat (chicken, mutton, or beef), cut into pieces - 1 large onion, thinly sliced - 2 tomatoes, chopped - 1/2 cup yogurt - 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste - 1 tsp biryani masala (or substitute with garam masala) - 1 tsp red chili powder - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp cumin seeds - 4-5 green chilies, slit - 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves - 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves - 1/2 cup fried onions (optional) - 4 cups water - 3 tbsp oil or ghee - Salt to taste - Lemon wedges (for garnish)
Instructions: 1. Prepare Rice: - Rinse basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain. 2. Cooking: - Heat oil or ghee in a large pot. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. - Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant. - Add tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and biryani masala. Cook until the tomatoes are soft. - Stir in meat and cook until browned. Add yogurt, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Cook until the meat is tender. - In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add the soaked rice and cook until 70% done. Drain the water. - Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat mixture. Sprinkle fried onions if using. - Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes. 3. Garnish and Serve: - Garnish with lemon wedges and serve hot. Enjoy your biryani!

Dal Tadka

Instructions: 1. Cook Dal: - Rinse the lentils and cook them with 2 cups of water in a pressure cooker until soft. Mash slightly and set aside. 2. Tadka: - Heat ghee or oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter. - Add onions and cook until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant. - Add tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft and the oil separates. - Add the cooked lentils to the pan and mix well. Adjust the consistency with water if needed. - Stir in garam masala and cook for a few minutes. 3. Garnish and Serve: - Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti. Enjoy your dal tadka!

Hi-tea

Pakora

Ingredients: - 1 cup gram flour (besan) - 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed) - 1 small onion, thinly sliced - 1 small potato, thinly sliced - 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped - 1/4 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp red chili powder - 1/4 tsp carom seeds (ajwain) - Salt to taste - Oil for frying - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
Instructions: Preparation: In a bowl, mix the gram flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, carom seeds, and salt. Add water gradually to form a thick batter. Add the sliced onions, potatoes, and green chilies to the batter and mix well. Frying: Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Drop spoonfuls of the batter into the hot oil. Fry until the pakoras are golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally. Remove the pakoras with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice. Enjoy your pakora!

Samosa

Ingredients: - 2 cups all-purpose flour - 1/4 cup oil - 1/2 tsp salt - Water (as needed) - 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed - 1/2 cup green peas, boiled - 1 small onion, finely chopped - 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped - 1/2 tsp cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp garam masala - Salt to taste - Oil for frying - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
Instructions: Preparation: In a bowl, mix the flour, salt, and oil. Add water gradually to form a stiff dough. Cover and set aside. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. When they start to splutter, add onions and green chilies. Sauté until onions are translucent. Add the mashed potatoes, boiled peas, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes. Let it cool. Assembly: Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle. Cut the circle in half to form two semi-circles. Fold each semi-circle into a cone and fill with the potato mixture. Seal the edges with water. Frying: Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice. Enjoy your samosa!

Mathri

Ingredients: - 2 cups all-purpose flour - 1/4 cup semolina (sooji) - 1/4 cup oil - 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds) - 1/2 tsp black pepper, coarsely crushed - Salt to taste - Water (as needed) - Oil for frying
Instructions: Preparation: In a bowl, mix the flour, semolina, ajwain, black pepper, and salt. Add oil and mix well. Gradually add water to form a stiff dough. Cover and set aside for 15-20 minutes. Rolling and Shaping: Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle. Prick the circles with a fork to prevent puffing up during frying. Frying: Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the mathris in batches until golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Serving: Serve the mathris at room temperature as a tea-time snack. Enjoy your mathri!

Murukku

Ingredients: - 2 cups rice flour - 1/2 cup urad dal flour - 1/4 cup melted butter - 1 tsp sesame seeds - 1/2 tsp cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing) - Salt to taste - Water (as needed) - Oil for frying
Instructions: Preparation: In a bowl, mix the rice flour, urad dal flour, sesame seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and salt. Add melted butter and mix well. Gradually add water to form a smooth, pliable dough. Shaping: Fill a murukku maker with the dough. Use the star-shaped nozzle. Press out murukkus onto a greased plastic sheet or directly into the hot oil in spiral shapes. Frying: Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the murukkus in batches until golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Serving: Let the murukkus cool completely and store in an airtight container. Serve as a crunchy snack. Enjoy your murukku!

Kachori

Ingredients: - 2 cups all-purpose flour - 1/4 cup oil - Salt to taste - Water (as needed) - 1 cup split urad dal, soaked and ground - 1 tsp fennel seeds - 1/2 tsp cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp asafoetida (hing) - 1/2 tsp red chili powder - 1/2 tsp garam masala - Oil for frying - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
Instructions: Preparation: In a bowl, mix the flour, salt, and oil. Add water gradually to form a stiff dough. Cover and set aside. In a pan, heat oil and add fennel seeds, cumin seeds, and asafoetida. When they start to splutter, add the ground urad dal. Cook until the mixture becomes dry. Add red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and let it cool. Assembly: Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a small circle. Place a spoonful of the urad dal mixture in the center and fold the edges over to form a ball. Flatten slightly. Frying: Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the kachoris until golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice. Enjoy your kachori!

Panipuri

Ingredients: - 1 cup semolina (sooji) - 2 tbsp all-purpose flour - 1/4 tsp baking soda - Water (as needed) - Oil for frying - 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes - 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas - 1 tsp chaat masala - 1/2 tsp cumin powder - Salt to taste - Tamarind chutney (for serving) - Green chutney (for serving) - Spicy water (prepared with mint, coriander, green chilies, and spices)
Instructions: Preparation: In a bowl, mix semolina, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and a pinch of salt. Add water gradually to form a stiff dough. Cover and set aside for 15-20 minutes. Shaping and Frying: Roll the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the circles until they puff up and turn golden brown. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. Filling: In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, boiled chickpeas, chaat masala, cumin powder, and salt. Assembly and Serving: Make a small hole in the center of each puri. Fill with the potato mixture, tamarind chutney, and green chutney. Serve immediately with spicy water. Enjoy your panipuri!

Popcorn

Ingredients: - 1/2 cup popcorn kernels - 2 tbsp oil - Salt to taste - 1/4 tsp turmeric powder (optional) - 1/4 tsp red chili powder (optional) - 1/4 tsp chaat masala (optional)
Instructions: Preparation: Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the popcorn kernels and cover with a lid. Popping: Shake the pot occasionally to prevent burning. Once the popping slows down, remove from heat. Seasoning: Add salt, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and chaat masala (if using). Mix well. Serving: Serve hot as a snack. Enjoy your popcorn!

Nachos

Ingredients: - 1 bag tortilla chips - 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or a blend) - 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained - 1/2 cup corn kernels - 1 small tomato, diced - 1 small onion, finely chopped - 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped - 1/4 cup sliced black olives - Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish) - Salsa (for serving) - Sour cream (for serving) - Guacamole (for serving)
Instructions: Preparation: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Assembly: Spread the tortilla chips on a baking sheet. Sprinkle shredded cheese, black beans, corn kernels, diced tomato, chopped onion, green chilies, and sliced black olives over the chips. Baking: Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with salsa, sour cream, and guacamole on the side. Enjoy your nachos!

Roasted Chickpeas

Ingredients: - 2 cups cooked chickpeas (canned or boiled) - 1 tbsp olive oil - 1/2 tsp paprika - 1/2 tsp garlic powder - 1/2 tsp cumin powder - 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional) - Salt to taste - Freshly ground black pepper to taste
Instructions: Preparation: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Pat the chickpeas dry with a paper towel. Seasoning: In a bowl, mix olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, cumin powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Add the chickpeas and toss to coat evenly. Roasting: Spread the chickpeas on a baking sheet in a single layer. Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, shaking the pan occasionally, until the chickpeas are crispy. Serving: Serve as a crunchy snack. Enjoy your roasted chickpeas!

Savory Muffins

Ingredients: - 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour - 1/2 cup grated cheese (cheddar or a blend) - 1/4 cup chopped spinach - 1/4 cup grated carrot - 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper - 1/4 cup chopped green onions - 1 tsp baking powder - 1/2 tsp baking soda - 1/2 tsp salt - 1/2 tsp black pepper - 1 cup buttermilk - 1/4 cup olive oil - 1 large egg
Instructions: Preparation: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease a muffin tin or line with paper muffin cups. Mixing: In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and black pepper. Add grated cheese, chopped spinach, grated carrot, chopped bell pepper, and green onions. Mix well. In another bowl, whisk together buttermilk, olive oil, and egg. Combining: Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined. Baking: Spoon the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 3/4 full. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Serving: Serve warm as a savory snack. Enjoy your savory muffins!

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients: - 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, broccoli, snap peas) - 1 tbsp vegetable oil - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 tbsp ginger, minced - 2 tbsp soy sauce - 1 tbsp hoisin sauce - 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional) - 1 tsp sesame oil - 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional) - Salt and pepper to taste - 1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (for thickening, optional) - Cooked rice or noodles for serving
Instructions: Preparation: Wash and chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Cooking: Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and ginger, and stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until they are tender-crisp. Stir in soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and oyster sauce (if using). Mix well. If using cornstarch, add it to the pan and stir until the sauce thickens. Cook for another 1-2 minutes. Drizzle with sesame oil and sprinkle with red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to taste. Serve: Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles. Enjoy your vegetable stir fry!

Grilled Salmon

Ingredients: - 4 salmon fillets - 2 tbsp olive oil - 2 tbsp lemon juice - 3 cloves garlic, minced - 1 tbsp fresh dill or parsley, chopped - 1 tsp paprika - Salt and pepper to taste - Lemon wedges for serving
Instructions: Preparation: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, chopped dill, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cooking: Brush the salmon fillets with the olive oil mixture. Place the salmon on the grill, skin-side down. Grill for 5-7 minutes per side, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork. Serve: Serve the grilled salmon with lemon wedges on the side. Enjoy your grilled salmon!

Rogan Josh

Ingredients: - 1 lb lamb, cut into cubes - 2 tbsp vegetable oil - 1 large onion, finely chopped - 4 cloves garlic, minced - 1 tbsp ginger, minced - 1 cup plain yogurt - 2 tbsp paprika - 2 tbsp ground coriander - 1 tbsp ground cumin - 1 tsp turmeric powder - 1 tsp garam masala - 1 tsp ground black pepper - 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat) - 1 cup beef or vegetable broth - Salt to taste - Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions: Preparation: Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until the onions are golden brown. Cooking: Add the lamb cubes and brown on all sides. Stir in paprika, ground coriander, ground cumin, turmeric, garam masala, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using). Cook for 2-3 minutes until fragrant. Reduce heat to low and mix in yogurt. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the broth and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook for 1.5-2 hours, or until the lamb is tender. Season with salt to taste. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan. Enjoy your Rogan Josh!

Pulao

Ingredients: - 1 cup basmati rice - 2 tbsp vegetable oil or ghee - 1 large onion, thinly sliced - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 inch ginger, minced - 1 tsp cumin seeds - 1 bay leaf - 1-2 cloves - 2-3 green cardamom pods - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp ground cumin - 1/2 tsp ground coriander - 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, beans) - 2 cups water - Salt to taste - Fresh coriander for garnish
Instructions: Preparation: Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside. Cooking: Heat oil or ghee in a pot over medium heat. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute. Add the onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are golden brown. Stir in turmeric powder, ground cumin, and ground coriander. Cook for a minute. Add the mixed vegetables and rice. Stir to coat the rice with the spices. Add water and salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed. Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot. Enjoy your Pulao!

Prawn Masala

Ingredients: - 1 lb prawns, peeled and deveined - 2 tbsp vegetable oil - 1 large onion, finely chopped - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 inch ginger, minced - 2 large tomatoes, chopped - 1/2 cup coconut milk - 1 tbsp coriander powder - 1 tsp cumin powder - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp red chili powder - 1/2 tsp garam masala - Salt to taste - Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions: Preparation: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are golden brown. Cooking: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they break down and become a thick paste. Stir in coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes. Add prawns and cook until they turn pink and are cooked through (about 5 minutes). Mix in coconut milk and garam masala. Simmer for another 2-3 minutes. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or bread. Enjoy your Prawn Masala!

Tofu Masala

Ingredients: - 1 block firm tofu, cubed - 2 tbsp vegetable oil - 1 large onion, finely chopped - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 inch ginger, minced - 2 large tomatoes, chopped - 1/2 cup plain yogurt - 1 tbsp coriander powder - 1 tsp cumin powder - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp red chili powder - 1/2 tsp garam masala - Salt to taste - Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions: Preparation: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are golden brown. Cooking: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn into a thick paste. Stir in coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes. Add cubed tofu and mix gently to coat with the spices. Stir in yogurt and garam masala. Simmer for 5 minutes until the tofu absorbs the flavors. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan. Enjoy your Tofu Masala!

Palak Paneer

Ingredients: - 200g paneer, cubed - 4 cups fresh spinach leaves - 2 tbsp vegetable oil - 1 large onion, finely chopped - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 inch ginger, minced - 2 large tomatoes, chopped - 1/2 cup heavy cream - 1 tsp cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp garam masala - 1/2 tsp red chili powder - Salt to taste - Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions: Preparation: Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water for 2 minutes. Drain and blend into a smooth puree. Cooking: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and sauté for a minute. Add onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are golden brown. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become a thick paste. Stir in turmeric powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 2 minutes. Add spinach puree and cook for 5 minutes. Gently fold in paneer cubes and mix well. Simmer for another 5 minutes. Stir in heavy cream and cook for 2 more minutes. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice or naan. Enjoy your Palak Paneer!

Lemon Rice

Ingredients: - 1 cup basmati rice - 2 tbsp vegetable oil - 1 tsp mustard seeds - 1/4 tsp turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp red chili powder - 1/4 cup peanuts - 2 green chilies, slit - 1 tbsp lemon juice - Salt to taste - Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions: Preparation: Rinse and cook the basmati rice. Set aside. Cooking: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add peanuts and slit green chilies. Sauté for a minute. Stir in turmeric powder and red chili powder. Cook for 30 seconds. Add the cooked rice and salt. Mix well. Stir in lemon juice and cook for another 2 minutes. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot. Enjoy your Lemon Rice!

Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients: - 500g chicken thighs, boneless and skinless - 3 tbsp olive oil - 2 tbsp lemon juice - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 tbsp ground cumin - 1 tbsp ground paprika - 1 tsp ground turmeric - 1 tsp ground coriander - 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon - 1/2 tsp ground allspice - Salt and pepper to taste - Pita bread or flatbread for serving - Sliced vegetables and tahini sauce for serving
Instructions: Preparation: In a bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, and all the spices. Add chicken thighs and marinate for at least 2 hours, preferably overnight. Cooking: Preheat a grill or oven to medium-high heat. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until fully cooked. Let it rest before slicing thinly. Serve: Serve sliced chicken shawarma in pita bread or flatbread with sliced vegetables and tahini sauce. Enjoy your Chicken Shawarma!

Sushi

Ingredients: - 2 cups sushi rice - 2 1/2 cups water - 1/2 cup rice vinegar - 2 tbsp sugar - 1 tsp salt - Nori (seaweed) sheets - 1 cucumber, sliced - 1 avocado, sliced - 1/2 lb sashimi-grade fish (tuna, salmon), sliced - Soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi for serving
Instructions: Preparation: Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Combine rice and water in a rice cooker and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions. In a small saucepan, heat rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved. Let cool. Once rice is cooked, transfer to a large bowl and gently mix in the vinegar mixture. Allow to cool. Assembling Sushi: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top. Arrange cucumber, avocado, and fish slices on the rice. Roll the sushi tightly using the mat, sealing the edge with a little water. Slice into bite-sized pieces. Serve: Serve sushi with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi. Enjoy your Sushi!

Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients: - 4 chicken breasts - 1 cup all-purpose flour - 2 large eggs, beaten - 1 cup breadcrumbs - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese - 1 cup marinara sauce - 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese - 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil or parsley - Olive oil for frying - Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions: Preparation: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Dredge each breast in flour, then dip in beaten eggs, and coat with a mixture of breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese. Cooking: Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry chicken breasts until golden brown on both sides (about 3-4 minutes per side). Transfer the chicken to a baking dish. Spoon marinara sauce over each piece and sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Baking: Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley before serving. Enjoy your Chicken Parmesan!

Mexican Tacos

Ingredients: - 1 lb ground beef or chicken - 1 tbsp vegetable oil - 1 onion, finely chopped - 2 cloves garlic, minced - 1 packet taco seasoning mix - 1/2 cup water - Taco shells or tortillas - Shredded lettuce - Diced tomatoes - Shredded cheese - Sour cream and salsa for serving - Fresh cilantro for garnish
Instructions: Preparation: Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic. Sauté until the onion is translucent. Cooking: Add ground beef or chicken and cook until browned and fully cooked. Stir in taco seasoning mix and water. Simmer for 5 minutes until the mixture thickens. Assemble Tacos: Fill taco shells or tortillas with the seasoned meat mixture. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, and salsa. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve. Enjoy your Mexican Tacos!
